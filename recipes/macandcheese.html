Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with a roux and adding milk, Cheddar, and Parmesan, resulting in a rich, decadent sauce that coats every nook and cranny of the noodles.

I've tweaked the amount of cheese and sour cream depending on what I've got in the fridge and it always turns out great! My mother has suggested this was made up as a way to use up ingredients in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Make the cheese sauce
  3. Make the topping
  4. Bake the mac and cheese
