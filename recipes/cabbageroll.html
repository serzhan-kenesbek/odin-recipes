Cabbage roll

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 pounds chopped cabbage
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cans beef broth

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Heat a large skillet
  3. Combine cabbage, tomato sauce, onion, rice
  4. Cover and bake in the preheated oven
